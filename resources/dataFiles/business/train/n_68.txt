Facebook's Sandberg Would Like to See Clinton President
HEADLINE_ARTICLE
Sheryl Sandberg, chief operating officer at Facebook Inc., and Richard Branson, chairman of Virgin Group Ltd., talk about gender equality in the workplace and politics, and the upcoming elections. They speak with "Bloomberg West" anchor Emily Chang at the Virgin Disruptors event yesterday. (The full interview will air tonight at 8:30 p.m. ET/PT as part of a special edition of "Bloomberg West" called "Balancing Act." Source: Bloomberg)
