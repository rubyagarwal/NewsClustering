The Year in Photos: U.S. Soccer in 2015
HEADLINE_ARTICLE
2015 is a loaded year for U.S. Soccer. The U.S. men's national team is coming off a World Cup year but has its sights set on capturing the Gold Cup to cement its place at the 2017 FIFA Confederations Cup. The U.S. women's national team eyes its first Women's World Cup title since 1999 in Canada. Youth national teams have World Cups in which to compete and gain valuable experience, and the U-23 sides are gunning for the 2016 Olympics in Rio. In photos, here is the year in U.S. Soccer.
