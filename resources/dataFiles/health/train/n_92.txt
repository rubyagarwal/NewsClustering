Are you clued up about tooth decay?

According to the latest Childrens Dental health Survey for England, Wales and Northern Ireland, nearly half of eight-year-olds and a third of five-year-olds have signs of decay in their milk teeth. Commissioned by the Health and Social Care Information Centre, the survey also suggest things have improved in the last 10 years, with children having fewer cavities overall.
